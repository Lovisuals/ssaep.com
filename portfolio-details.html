From Wasteland to Wealth

Before SSAEP intervention, the 1/4 plot behind the Physics laboratory was overgrown with weeds. The school lacked a functional space for Agricultural Science practicals.

The Solution

Blessed Farms partnered with the school administration to implement a rigorous 3-term plan:

  • Term 1: Land clearing and installation of 2 mobile fish ponds (500 capacity each).
  • Term 2: Planting of Fluted Pumpkin (Ugu) and Maize using drip irrigation.
  • Term 3: Student-led harvest and sales to local market women (Offtake system).

"Our students now look forward to Agric classes. The practical exposure has improved their grades significantly."

Mr. A. Johnson

Principal, Ota Comprehensive
