Executive Summary

Bridging the gap between theory and practical application in Nigerian Schools.

The Challenge Facing Schools

The Gap: The National Agriculture curriculum emphasizes practical skills, entrepreneurship, and technology. However, many schools lack the technical manpower, farm structures, and practical exposure required to meet these demands.

The Solution: SSAEP solves this by providing trained instructors, practical farm activities, and modern farming technologies. We run throughout the academic year, seamlessly integrating into your school timetable to cover crop production, livestock, and soilless farming.

By partnering with Blessed Farms & Agro Services, your institution benefits from:

  • Improved Performance: Target a 30% increase in student competence scores for BECE, WAEC, and NECO practicals.
  • Revenue Generation: Establishment of a profitable school farm with transparent record-keeping.
  • Teacher Empowerment: Capacity building sessions for your existing Agriculture teachers.
  • Opportunity Access: Connecting students to scholarships from IITA, Soilless Farm Lab, and other empowerment schemes.

Risk Management & Safeguarding

We prioritize the safety of students and the success of the farm. Here is how we manage common concerns.

1. What happens if there is crop failure?

We mitigate this by using drought-resistant seed varieties, implementing irrigation systems, and staggering planting schedules to ensure continuous harvest.

2. How do you ensure student safety?

Safety is paramount. All instructors undergo background checks and safeguarding training. We strictly supervise all livestock units and tool usage. Students are not involved in hazardous tasks, and First-Aid kits are always available.

3. Will this disrupt academic activities?

No. The program is designed to integrate seamlessly into the school's existing timetable for Agricultural Science practicals. We work with the school administration to plan sessions effectively.

4. What about livestock mortality?

We maintain strict biosecurity measures, proper vaccination schedules, and optimal stocking density to keep livestock healthy and productive.
